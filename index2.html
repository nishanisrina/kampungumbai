"Talian Kasih" COVID-19

Take care everyone!, July 21th, 2021


Assalammualaikum and a good night We are sorry for the late timing of publishing our new entry. The admin (me) was busy for the whole day. Anyway, we have received the news about the amount of COVID-19 cases here in our country like usual. And the total are 11,985 cases. As for Melaka state, there are 453 cases . Please stay calm, and stay positive. Insya-Allah, we can do this. Make sure to follow all the Standard Operating Procedure (S.O.P). Stay home and do not go outside or leave the house if there is no any important thing to do. Tonight, we want to share the care line or also known as the "Talian Kasih" which you can contact for counseling.

Call 15999 or Whatsapp 019-2615999

Please take care everyone. We hope this entry helps you. Let's continue our fight always. #KitaJagaKita

Happy Eid Adha

"Salam Eid Adha"!, July 20th, 2021


Assalammualaikum and happy Eid Adha to all muslims who are reading this now! We are feeling so excited while typing this since finally another day of celebration has come. How was your day today? We are sorry that we could not post entry a little bit earlier today like usual but yeah, we are here now hehe. Everybody was having fun today eventhough we cannot celebrate at outside of our house like we used to. This is our second year to celebrate while staying at home. Everybody's routine today was like :

  • Wear beautiful baju raya
  • Take photo with family
  • Post on social medias the photos with "Happy Eid Adha from us" wish
  • Video call with other siblings or family
  • Eat
And goes back to normal routine. Is it the same for you? If not then it is alright! Just stay calm and just enjoy your day. Thank you for reading our entry. We will try to post as much as we can so you guys will enjoy coming here ((= Bye everyone and stay safe!

What To Do On Day of Arafah

Here what you can do!, July 19th, 2021


Assalammualaikum and happy Monday. As we all now, today is Arafah Day for the muslims. We will be sharing with you five things that you can do today. Make sure to do them okay (=

  1. Fast. "It expiates the sins of the past year and the coming year." (Muslim)
  2. Plan for your workship now. Schedule Quran, Zikir, Duas and Charity in.
  3. Sunnah of Rasulullah SAW to recite the Tahleel, Takbeer and Tahmeed as many times as possible.
  4. Seek forgiveness, make loads of Dua. Especially, between asar and maghrib, before you break the fast.
  5. Don't do minimum. Push yourself to make the most of this blessed day.
That is all from us today. Thank your for reading and have a good day ahead! See you all in our next entry!
