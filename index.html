Tips for your business

Some tips!, July 23th, 2021


Assalammualaikum and good morning everyone! We hope that you all are having a great day today. Since we all know that nowadays a lot of us have started or already into business, we want to share three tips regarding this.

  1. Improve your customer service to amaze your customers
    2. Customer service is not only about communicating. You can gift them a freegift like a small handsanitizer, handcream or something that will be useful for them. This will not only amaze your customers but also make them feel enjoyable to buy from you! Remember, customer is always right! (=

  2. Build better customer relationship to retain more long-term clients
    3. Try to contact the customers again and share the latest promotion or inform them about the upcoming product or new product in the store. And don't forget about your loyal customers! Give them a special discount or freegift or maybe a voucher for them to re-purchase your product! Remember to always showcyour appreciate to them!

  3. Optimize your website to grow your profits
    4. If your product is high demand and you think that answering Whatsapp messages or chats on other social media are starting to burnden you, we highly suggest for you to create a website so your customers can buy there smoothly without actually disturbing your time! Besides, having your own website store makes you look a lot more professional and trustable.

Thank you for reading our new entry. Lastly, here we want to share a quote by Wayne Huizenga.
People dream of success, while other people get up every morning and make it happen

Stay safe everyone and we hope that our new entry will help you!
