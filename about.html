ABOUT US



Situated in Mukim Umbai Jasin District Melaka, Kampung Umbai is a traditional village where it is the main village of 6 localities that includes Umbai Pasal, Umbai Tengah, Umbai Baruh, Umbai Sungai Sembilang, Pekan Umbai and Umbai Baru with a population about 2500 people lives here.

Kampung Umbai with a motto "Keterampilan Maju Berilmu" is an active village that always up to date with current issues and frequently updating activities and information regarding Kampung Umbai in social media including Facebook and blog inline with current trend where people using gadgets to get informations.

KAMPUNG UMBAI MISSION IS TO BE:

✔ Tourism Center
✔ Grilled Fish Center
✔ Agricultural Development Center
✔ Development of Religious Education
✔ Center of Customs and Culture & Academic and Skills
